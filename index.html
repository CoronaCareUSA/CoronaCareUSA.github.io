Meet CoronaCare!

We are working day and night to bring you Corona Care USA, a platform that will connect people who need and can give financial help ...one bag of groceries at a time.

So how will it work?

People who need help will create a short profile with the cost of their upcoming groceries. People who can help will select a person or family’s profile and pay for their upcoming groceries. Corona Care will email the person or family in need a virtual grocery gift card.

So what’s taking so long?

We’re forming partnerships with grocery store chains across the country. We’re registering as a charity in all 50 states. And we’re ensuring our website is a seamless experience. But we’ll be ready to connect people in the next few weeks!
